📖 SourceFinder Complete Guide

Everything you need to find real factory suppliers with WeChat contacts

🚀 Quick Start (30 seconds)

  1. Type the Brand Name — e.g. Nike, LV, Supreme
  2. Type the Product Keywords — e.g. Tech Fleece, belt, Dunk Low
  3. Leave platform on All-in-One and hit Search
  4. Green WeChat IDs are factory contacts — click to copy instantly
  5. Add them on WeChat, ask for catalog and pricing

🎯 Platform Guide

⚡ All-in-One — Best for beginners. Casts the widest net across all platforms.
🖼️ Yupoo — Highest hit rate for reps. Suppliers post full catalogs, WeChat always in bio.
🎵 Douyin — Chinese TikTok. Suppliers post product videos. WeChat in their bio.
🏭 1688 — Real wholesale/factory prices. No WeChat usually — chat through platform.
📱 Weidian — WeChat-linked stores. Small sellers, low MOQ, easy to contact.
📕 XiaoHongShu — Chinese Instagram. Fashion suppliers constantly posting here.
👟 Dewu/Poizon — Best for passing quality sneakers. Putian factory contacts.
🛒 Pinduoduo — Factory direct pricing. Some sellers have WeChat in shop description.

🔍 Pro Tips

Always fill Brand field — activates brand-lock. "LV" in brand rejects any non-LV results automatically.
Be specific — "Jordan 4 Thunder" beats "Jordan shoes". Include colorway, model number.
Load More — best factory contacts are often on page 2-3. Always click Load More.
WeChat Only toggle — filters to only show results with confirmed WeChat IDs.
Image Search — upload any product photo and Baidu will find which factories make it.
Deep Scan — visits each page, reads QR codes and images to find hidden WeChats. Slow but thorough.
Try multiple platforms — same search on Yupoo vs Douyin returns completely different suppliers.

✅ Passing Mode

Click Passing tab for NFC/passing quality. Uses Chinese keywords like 过验, NFC芯片, 1:1, 莆田 that target export-quality factories.

过验 — Passes authentication
NFC芯片 — Has NFC chip
莆田 — Putian factories
1:1/同厂 — Same factory

💬 Messaging Suppliers

你好，我想买[品牌][产品]，能发图片和价格吗？谢谢

"Hello, I want to buy [brand][product], can you send pictures and price? Thank you"

❌ Common Mistakes

Leaving Brand field empty — disables brand-lock, mixed irrelevant results
Too generic keywords — "shoes" or "bag" gives useless results
Only checking page 1 — click Load More, best contacts are deeper
Ignoring Yupoo results — almost always have WeChat in bio
