📖 How to Use Cade's SourceFinder

1. Searching for Suppliers

Enter a Brand Name (e.g. Nike) and Product Keywords (e.g. Calm Slides). Hit Search. The app queries Baidu — China's largest search engine — using specialized factory-finding keywords in Chinese. Results show supplier listings with WeChat IDs you can contact directly.

2. Choosing a Platform

All-in-One — searches everything at once (recommended)
Yupoo — best for passing quality, factories post full catalogs here
1688 — wholesale pricing direct from factories
Weidian — smaller sellers, good for reps
Baidu — broadest search, most results

3. Passing vs Rep Mode

Use the Passing tab for passing quality items — the search uses different Chinese keywords (原厂, 出口转内销, 1:1开模) that surface export-quality factory contacts instead of typical rep sellers. Middlemen listed in Passing results are often legit — some have exclusive factory relationships.

4. Deep Scan + WeChat IDs

Toggle Deep Scan to have the app visit every result page and extract WeChat IDs from images, QR codes and text. Toggle WeChat Only to filter results to only show listings with WeChat contact info. Click any highlighted WeChat ID (shown in green) to copy it instantly.

5. Tips & Best Practices

• Be specific with keywords — "Jordan 4 Thunder" beats "Jordan shoes"
• Add a brand name AND product for best accuracy
• Try multiple platforms for the same item
• WeChats that appear on multiple results = more trustworthy
• Save results using the Saved tab
• Use the Finds tab to track contacts you've already reached out to
• Issues? Contact Cade immediately.
