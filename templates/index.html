📖 SourceFinder — Complete Guide

Everything you need to find real factory suppliers with WeChat contacts

🚀 Quick Start (30 seconds)

  1. Type the Brand Name — e.g. Nike, LV, Supreme
  2. Type the Product Keywords — e.g. Tech Fleece, belt, Dunk Low
  3. Leave platform on All-in-One and hit Search
  4. Green WeChat IDs are factory contacts — click any to copy instantly
  5. Add them on WeChat, ask for catalog and pricing

🎯 Platform Guide — Which to Use

⚡ All-in-OneBEST FOR BEGINNERS
Searches Baidu broadly. Widest net. Use this first every time.
🖼️ YupooBEST HIT RATE FOR REPS
Suppliers post full photo catalogs. WeChat always in bio. Try this if All-in-One doesn't work.
🎵 DouyinBEST FOR VIDEO CATALOGS
Chinese TikTok. Suppliers post product videos. WeChat is in their bio. Great for finding new factories.
🏭 1688
Real wholesale factory prices. Usually no WeChat — contact through the platform chat instead.
📱 Weidian
WeChat-linked mini stores. Small sellers, low MOQ, easy to contact directly.
📕 XiaoHongShu
Chinese Instagram. Fashion suppliers post here constantly. DM or grab WeChat from posts.
👟 Dewu/Poizon
Best for passing quality sneakers. Searches for Putian factory contacts specifically.

🔍 Pro Search Tips

Always fill Brand field — activates brand-lock. Searching "LV" in brand field rejects any non-LV result automatically.
Be specific — "Jordan 4 Thunder" beats "Jordan shoes". Include colorway or model number.
Load More — best factory contacts are often on page 2-3. Always hit Load More after first results.
WeChat Only toggle — filters to only show listings with confirmed WeChat IDs. Saves time.
Image Search button — upload any product photo, Baidu reverse searches to find factories making that exact item.
Deep Scan — visits each result page, reads QR codes to find hidden WeChats. Slower but finds more.
Try multiple platforms — same search on Yupoo vs Douyin returns different suppliers every time.

✅ Passing Mode — NFC Quality

Click the Passing tab to search for passing-quality/NFC factories using specialized Chinese keywords: 过验, NFC芯片, 1:1, 莆田, 同厂. These target export-quality factories that produce goods passing retail authentication.

过验 — Passes authentication
NFC芯片 — Has NFC chip like retail
莆田 — Putian city sneaker factories
1:1/同厂 — Same factory as retail

💬 What to Say to Suppliers

你好，我想买[品牌][产品]，能发图片和价格吗？谢谢

"Hello, I want to buy [brand][product], can you send pictures and price? Thank you"

  • Ask for 产品图片 (product photos) — real photos not stock images
  • Ask for 价格 (price) and minimum order quantity
  • Ask for 质量/批次 (quality/batch) — which version
  • Ask about 国际物流 (international shipping)

❌ Common Mistakes

Leaving Brand field empty — disables brand-lock, you get random irrelevant results
Too generic keywords — "shoes" or "bag" returns useless results. Be specific.
Only checking page 1 — click Load More every time, best contacts are buried deeper
Ignoring Yupoo results — they almost always have WeChat in the bio, click Open Page
