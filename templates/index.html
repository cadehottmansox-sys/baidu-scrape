📖 How to Find Suppliers on Douyin

🏭 Factory Search Tips
  • Search: 品牌 + 厂家 + 微信 (brand + factory + wechat)
  • Add 莆田 for Putian sneaker factories
  • Add 一手货源 for first-hand supply
  • Look for accounts with 直播 (live stream)
  • High follower count = more established
⚡ Passing Search Tips
  • Search: 品牌 + 纯原 (pure original)
  • Add PK版/OG版/LJR版 for batch codes
  • Look for 过验 (passes authentication)
  • Check video comments for WeChat
  • DM accounts with 联系方式 in bio
💰 Negotiation Tips
  • Always ask for 价格表 (price list)
  • Request 实物图 (real product photos)
  • Negotiate MOQ — factories prefer bulk
  • Ask about 快递包邮 (shipping included)
  • Pay via WeChat Pay, avoid Alipay strangers
🚫 Red Flags
  • Asks for full payment before QC photos
  • No product videos, only stock images
  • Price way below market (too good = fake)
  • Refuses video call / live demo
  • Pushes you to pay via personal bank
